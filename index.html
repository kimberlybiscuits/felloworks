How trust works

Trust is the product.

Entry vetting

Invite-only to start. Every member is vouched for by someone already inside. That vouching carries weight in a small community. More than any star rating.

Real profiles

Portfolio, work samples, testimonials, professional context. Enough to make a proper judgement instead of a snap decision based on a headshot and a bio.

Talk first

The conversation comes first: working style, availability, how they handle a difficult brief.

Honest feedback

After a collaboration, both sides answer a short set of questions on the things that matter: did they deliver, did they communicate, would you bring them back.
